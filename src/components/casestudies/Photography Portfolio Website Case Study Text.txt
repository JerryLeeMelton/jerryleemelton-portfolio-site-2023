Photography Portfolio Website


Challenge: Create a portfolio website that can showcase the different sides of my photography skills.

Solution: A clean, simple website with responsive design.



Starting Point

Since 2014, I created my photography portfolio website using Wordpress.  There were many nice things about Wordpress, but after a while it felt like overkill for my simple website and I wanted to try my hand at designing something from scratch.  

One thing that I was interested in was keeping the design very clean and minimal.  I wanted to explore this idea further later, but my initial feeling was that a minimalistic design in a portfolio website helps keep the focus on the work being presented rather than on the design of the site itself.
